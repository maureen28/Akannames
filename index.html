Akan Names

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, oran ancestor's middle name.This naming tradition is shared throughout West Africa and the African diaspora.
